# 📈 Rossmann Sales Prediction App 🛒 Welcome to the Rossmann Sales Prediction App! 🚀 This application leverages SARIMA and Prophet models to predict monthly and weekly sales up to a year based on the Rossman Sales data. ## SARIMA To predict sales using SARIMA, select the store type, store ID, and choose whether you want to display weekly or monthly sales predictions. Enter the end prediction date, and the app will provide you with historical sales data and predicted sales data until the specified end date. ## Prophet To predict sales using PROPHET, select the store ID, and choose the duration for which you want to forecast sales beginning from a date. Choose the dates in that duration for which you want to offer a promotion and the days that are going to be holidays.Based on the historical data, the app will forecast sales for the specified duration. Explore sales trends, make informed decisions, and plan for the future using the power of predictive analytics! 📊💡 Feel free to navigate through the app and make predictions based on different scenarios. Happy forecasting! 🌐🔮
